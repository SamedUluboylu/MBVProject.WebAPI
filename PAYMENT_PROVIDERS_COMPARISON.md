## Detailed Provider Analysis

### Stripe
**Pros:**
- Global reach with 135+ currencies
- Excellent documentation and developer tools
- Built-in fraud detection
- Supports subscriptions, one-time payments
- Fast settlement (2-7 days)

**Cons:**
- Higher fees for international cards
- Requires business verification
- Limited support for some regions

**When to use:**
- Selling to international customers
- Need advanced features (subscriptions, marketplaces)
- Want best-in-class developer experience

---

### Iyzico
**Pros:**
- #1 payment provider in Turkey
- Local support in Turkish
- Supports installment payments
- Fast approval process
- Competitive rates

**Cons:**
- Primarily for Turkish market
- Documentation mostly in Turkish
- Fewer features than Stripe

**When to use:**
- Targeting Turkish customers
- Need installment payment options
- Want local payment methods (BKM Express)

---

### PayTR
**Pros:**
- Turkish payment solution
- Competitive pricing
- Good local support
- Simple integration

**Cons:**
- Limited to Turkey
- Fewer features than competitors
- Basic reporting tools

**When to use:**
- Turkish-only business
- Cost-conscious merchants
- Simple payment needs

---

### PayPal
**Pros:**
- Trusted brand worldwide
- Buyer protection
- Easy for customers (no card needed)
- Quick setup

**Cons:**
- Higher fees for micro-transactions
- Can freeze accounts
- Disputes favor buyers

**When to use:**
- Digital products
- International marketplace
- Customers prefer PayPal

---

### Cryptocurrency
**Pros:**
- Lowest fees (1%)
- No chargebacks
- Global without borders
- Privacy-focused

**Cons:**
- Price volatility
- Technical complexity
- Limited adoption
- Regulatory uncertainty

**When to use:**
- Tech-savvy audience
- Want to avoid chargebacks
- International payments with low fees

---

### Cash on Delivery
**Pros:**
- Zero setup
- Trust factor for new shops
- No online payment fees
- Popular in emerging markets

**Cons:**
- Risk of order cancellation
- Collection logistics
- Cash handling costs

**When to use:**
- New business building trust
- Local delivery only
- Customers prefer cash

---

### Bank Transfer
**Pros:**
- Lowest/no fees
- Direct to bank
- Good for B2B
- Large transactions

**Cons:**
- Manual verification needed
- Slower order processing
- Customer friction

**When to use:**
- High-value orders
- B2B transactions
- Cost-sensitive business

## Decision Matrix

Choose based on:

1. **Your market:** Local vs. International
2. **Product type:** Physical vs. Digital
3. **Average order value:** Small vs. Large
4. **Technical capability:** Simple vs. Advanced
5. **Setup urgency:** Immediate vs. Can wait
6. **Budget:** Fee-sensitive vs. Feature-focused
